Trophies and Crowns

A Primarch is to the Kingdom of Long as a god is to his church. That is, the epitome of power and an ideal to strive toward.
The throne is held by a single individual, with which the power to command any function of the government resides. This individual, the Primarch, can be any citizen, land owner, duke or duchess from the national populus. The identity of this Primarch is public knowledge, including the story of how they reached the position they're in and how they fared in "The Games"

The Games are a series of trials and contests, conducted in the capital city of "Long". The competitors of the games are champions chosen by each duke/duchess of the individual provinces, potentially including the duke/duchess themselves.
Once chosen, these champions head to the capital where they are hailed as heroes; given feasts and amenities many only could dream of. This treatment, of course, is short-lived as each of these champions risk their lives with each challenge they take on.
