 Djinni control large palaces on the floating isles of the plane of air. Using servants to populate and preserve their comforts, these palaces are often accepting of those looking to serve great masters.

Aarakocra are common among the islands, but also commonly join together to make use of airships, or rarely spelljammers, to travel as traders, mercenaries, or pirates both in the plane of air and traveling to the material plane through well charted planar crossings.

Cloud Giants live at the highest peaks of the tallest mountains, usually stretching from the prime material into the plane of air. These giants give little thought to the politics of the plane of air, favoring control of those below them in the ordning over trifling with the djinni. 		Water, Ice, Ash, Mud, Fire Earth, Magma Interplanar Highway: Due to a combination of the ease of travel and the frequency of natural planar crossings, the plane of air has become a popular trade route for those looking to safely move between planes. As such, many of the charted gates are claimed by guilds who charge a fee to travel through a route they protect and upkeep.

Luxury of the void: The floating islands dotting the infinite emptiness are inhabited by a wide array of creatures. The Djinni generally have control of large clusters of islands, but between Djinni land lie islands claimed by Aarakocra, Owlin, or even Hadozee.
